About Me

Hello!

Welcome to my blog! I'm Virginie Bonhomme, a New York City-based artist, developer, and cybersecurity enthusiast. My journey into the tech world started from a creative perspective when I developed "Duende," a 3D children’s book that sparked my interest in merging art with technology. This led me to pursue a coding bootcamp, followed by extensive self-learning in design and user experience.

As my technical skills evolved, I became deeply fascinated with cybersecurity, recognizing its vital role in safeguarding our data amidst the growing prominence of AI tools. My artistic background uniquely positions me to explore the creative potential and inherent risks of technology. I am dedicated to employing these tools safely and responsibly, aiming to make cybersecurity intuitive and accessible for everyone.

Through earning my Google Cybersecurity certification and engaging in numerous practical labs, I have tackled real-world security challenges. These experiences are documented on my GitHub, showcasing the strategies and methodologies I apply. I aim to blend effective security measures with engaging user experiences, focusing on projects that enhance data protection and educate users.

Here on my blog, you’ll find insights, tools, and tips for securing your digital world. Join me as we explore, learn, and contribute to creating safer digital spaces. Let's connect and collaborate to push the boundaries of cybersecurity in innovative and user-friendly ways.
